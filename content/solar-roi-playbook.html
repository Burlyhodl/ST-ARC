Solar ROI Playbook Checklist for Daily Execution

A reliable checklist keeps teams grounded. The solar roi playbook checklist below reinforces consistent habits:

  • Confirm lead source, qualification status, and expected close probability in the CRM.
  • Update site survey data with geotagged photos and measurement notes within 24 hours.
  • Validate design simulations against historical utility consumption and weather data.
  • Review proposal assumptions, including degradation rates, financing fees, and incentive expiration timelines.
  • Schedule pre-install coordination calls with customers and subcontractors to confirm access and safety requirements.
  • Document post-installation walkthroughs, monitor performance dashboards, and schedule follow-up touchpoints.

Teams that adhere to this checklist maintain momentum, deliver predictable outcomes, and reinforce customer trust.
